Manifest Networks is a network and security automation solution for multi-cloud application deployment. An advanced, topology aware IPAM module ensures that IP addresses of endpoints (VMs or pods) align with the topology of your network. This in turn makes it possible to effectively aggregate routes, resulting in many operational and security advantages:

  • Ability to use native L3 routing, allowing network equipment to work at its best
  • Greatly reduced number of routes in your networking hardware
  • Stable routing configurations, less route updates required
  • No "leaking" of endpoint routes into the networking fabric

Key to this operation is the topology manifest which describes the underlying network topology of your multi-cloud cluster envrionment.

This web based manifest generator makes describing your network toplogy simple. It will ask you a few questions about your network and then give you a link from which to download your manifest.
